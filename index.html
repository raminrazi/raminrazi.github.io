About

I am a research scientist at Rakuten Institute of Technology (RIT). I did my PhD in the EECS Department of UC Merced, under supervision of Prof. Paul Maglio and Prof. Harish Bhat. I received my MSc in Artificial Intelligence form Sharif Univeristy of Technology and my BSc in Computer Engineering from Iran University of Science and Technology.

My research is focused on machine learning and its applications in computer vision, image processing, time series, recommendation systems, etc.
